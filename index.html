Bawaseer (Piles) Ka Gharelu Ilaj - 5 Asaan Upay

Disclaimer: Ye jankari sirf samanya gyaan ke liye hai. Kisi bhi ilaj se pehle doctor se salah zaroor lein.

Dosto, bawaseer ek aam samasya hai jo galat khan-paan aur kabz ki wajah se hoti hai. Aaj hum aapko 5 gharelu upay batayenge jo dard aur jalan me turant aaram dete hain.

1. Garam Paani Se Sikai Karein

Raat ko sone se pehle 10-15 minute tak garam paani me baithein. Isse soojan kam hoti hai aur dard me aaram milta hai. Paani zyada garam na ho, gunguna hona chahiye.

2. Anjeer Ka Sevan

2 anjeer ko raat bhar paani me bhigo dein. Subah khaali pet anjeer kha lein aur paani pee lein. Ye kabz door karta hai jo bawaseer ka mukhya karan hai.

3. Nariyal Tel Lagayein

Shudh nariyal tel ko rui ki madad se prabhavit sthaan par lagayein. Din me 2-3 baar lagane se jalan aur khujli me aaram milta hai.

4. Chhach (Buttermilk) Piyein

Khane ke baad ek glass chhach me bhuna jeera aur kala namak daal kar piyein. Ye pachan theek karta hai aur pet saaf rakhta hai.

5. Zyada Paani Piyein

Din bhar me kam se kam 8-10 glass paani piyein. Paani ki kami se mal sakht ho jata hai jisse takleef badhti hai.

Doctor Se Kab Milein?

Agar upar bataye upay se 7 din me aaram na mile, ya khoon zyada aa raha ho to turant doctor se sampark karein.
